sponsor space

Designed and built a pop-up sponsor space from scratch for We Love Customers.
GitHub >

We Love Customers is a web marketing agency specialized in sponsorship. They offer sponsor spaces (on request!) accessible directly on the client's website. In order to add this functionality to their catalog, I had the opportunity to create a brand new generic sponsor space in the form of a pop-up. This space was design to contain an area for missions, rewards, sponsorships and a “how does it work?” section. ". It is currently in use and online with several clients.
