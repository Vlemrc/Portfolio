Designed and built a showcase website from scratch for Le Dressing Bayeux during the Covid period.

I undertook the development of a showcase website for 'Le Dressing,' a small women's ready-to-wear boutique in Bayeux (14) following the challenges posed by the Covid-19 crisis. Given the boutique's modest size, the website aimed at revitalizing customer engagement without imposing a significant workload. Opting for a one-page design, I ensured the site reflected the boutique's essence and values. While successfully online for a year, the owners decided to close it post-Covid, deeming it unnecessary in the return to normalcy. Although the site is no longer active, you can refer to the initial prototype, created before the owner's content additions.
