le dressing bayeux

Designed and built a showcase website from scratch for Le Dressing Bayeux during the Covid period.
GitHub >

Le Dressing is a small women's ready-to-wear boutique in Bayeux (14). I created their showcase site after the difficult times linked to the covid crisis to bring back customers. It's a small shop. Therefore, the site should not be large and above all not require a lot of manpower. It had to reflect the DNA of the store and its values. So I decided to do a one page. The site was online for a year before closing its doors, the managers no longer seeing the usefulness of it with the return to normal life, post-covid. As the site is no longer online, I refer you here to the first model developed even before the manager completed it.
